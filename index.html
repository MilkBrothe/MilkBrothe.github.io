The YoYo Community

I was shocked by how large the community of throwers is when I started out in the hobby. There are a handful of facebook groups (I'm actively on this oneThe Yoyo BST & Talk), a subreddit (r/throwers), and a substantial number of forums that are very active. The overall attitude of the community is very positive. The yoyo players I've had the pleasure of meeting, or talking to online have been pleasant, if not outright cheery. It's a shared passion, in my opinion.

Yoyo Brands!

Boutique brands

From what I have learned there are a few different kinds of yoyo brands in general. The first I would probably call either boutique, or artisenal. Companies like CLYW, One Drop, MonkeyFingeR Designs, and G-Squared fall into this category. Generally speaking they yoyo's botique companies make are more artistic interpretations of the design principles, (arguably higher quality), and are made in batch runs (i.e. not mass produced). They tend to be more expensive, but in my opinion it's worth the extra bit. (fun fact! The moto One Drop started with, and still maintains is 'metal for all'. Meaning their goal is to make enough high quality metal yoyo's at an affordable price that anybody who wants one can get one.)

Mass produced

The other group of companies are mass-producers. Brands like Yomega, and YoyoFactory fall into this category. I would hesitate to say they produce inferior quality, but also wouldn't put them on the same level as One Drop or MonkeyFingeR in those terms.
