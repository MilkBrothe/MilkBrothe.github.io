Rain City Skills: The Loonie

A canadian producer of return tops (they can't call them yoyos for legal reasons) named Rain City Skills put out a micro-throw called The Loonie. I'm not really sure what to say outside of that the thing is super-duper tiny, and even on the slightly heavier side for modern yoyo specs. It's fun, and a real challenge to actually land any complex tricks on successfully.

Excellence Below
Yoyo Specs
Width: 26 mm
Diameter: 32 mm
Gap Width: 4.34mm
Bearing Size: Size A
Base Weight: 65.40 grams
Axel System: Tapped
Material: Brass
