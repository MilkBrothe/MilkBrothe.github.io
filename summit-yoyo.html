One Drop Yoyos x CLYW

The Summit

In 2015 a canadian company named CLYW, and One Drop yoyo's collaborated to produce a yoyo that represented both companies. The result was The Summit; in my humble opinion one of the finest mono-metal yoyo's ever sold

Yoyo Specs
Width: 46mm
Diameter: 55.64mm
Gap Width: 4.34mm
Bearing Size: Large (C) Stainless Steel
Base Weight: 64.20mm
Axel System: Side Effects
Finish: Pyramatte
Material: 6061 Aluminium
