One Drop Yoyos x CLYW

The Summit

In 2015 a canadian company named CLYW, and One Drop yoyo's collaborated to produce a yoyo that represented both companies. The result was The Summit. The balance between comfort on the catch, spin time/speed, and stability is unreal. This thing is just... fun. It's kind of difficult to come up with much else to say otherwise, honestly.

One Drop YoYos

Yoyo Specs
Width: 46mm
Diameter: 55.64mm
Gap Width: 4.34mm
Bearing Size: Large (C) Stainless Steel
Base Weight: 64.20 grams
Axel System: Side Effects
Finish: Pyramatte
Material: 6061 Aluminium
