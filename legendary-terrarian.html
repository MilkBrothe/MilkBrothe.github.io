One Drop Yoyos x CLYW

The Legendary Terrarian

One Drop dropped their first run of Legendary Terrarians in their shop on June 13th of 2019. It's a modified version of the original with a denser aluminium alloy that changes how thin the walls can be machined, and allows for different distribution of weight.

Excellence Below
Yoyo Specs
Width: 46mm
Diameter: 55mm
Gap Width: 4.34mm
Bearing Size: Large (C) Stainless Steel
Base Weight: 60.70mm
Axel System: Side Effects
Finish: Pyramatte
Material: 7075 Aluminium
