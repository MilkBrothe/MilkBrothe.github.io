YoyoCommunity: The Spectrum

The spectrum is the signature yoyo of the company Yoyocommunity, and its founder Tony Alonso. This was their first design from the ground-up, and the care they put into it shows. It's smooth, gorgeous, and the undercut rims are absolutely magnificent. In play it feels almost more like a bi-metal (yoyo with heavy rims) than a mono-metal, and has enough spin time to back up that assertion. The Spectrum is easily one of my favorites, and I would love to have had the opportunity to snag a few of their other throws, but YoyoCommunity unfortuantely closed its doors for business several years ago, though their website is still up online, so I will put a link to it below.
